Thor

Bio

Thor is one of the original Avengers. Thor is the Norse God of thunder and lightning and he wields a mystical hammer known as Mjolnir. Only people deemed worthy can wield this hammer, making Thor one of the only people able to lift it. Thor comes from another world called Asgard. The Asgardians are known to be a powerful race, known for being honorable and brave warriors. Thor's father is Odin, the ruler and protector of Asgard, while his brother is Loki, one of the main villains to the Avengers. During Thor's time on Earth he has fallen in love with a scientist named Jane.

Abilities

  • Super strength
  • Skilled in hand-to-hand combat
  • Highly resistant to injury
  • Able to summon and shoot lightning from his hammer

Villains

  • Loki
  • Malekith
  • Ultron
  • Thanos
