Black Widow

Bio

Black Widow is a skilled agent in the organization known as S.H.I.E.L.D. She is also known as Natasha Romanova. She is an elite spy and assassin. She was born in Russia and was trained by the KGB's Red Room Academy since she was a little girl. After leaving Russia and coming to the United States, she became a top agent for S.H.I.E.L.D. She has a close relationship with Hawkeye.

Abilities

  • Excellent marksman
  • Skilled in hand-to-hand combat
  • Above average reflexes
  • Uses stun technology

Villains

  • Loki
  • Ultron
  • Thanos
